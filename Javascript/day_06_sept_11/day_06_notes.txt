Strings
=======
-strings are used to represent and manipulate a sequence of characters.



String methods:
--------------
length;
toUpperCase();
toLowerCase();
charAt();
at()
charCodeAt();
concat();
indexOf();
lastIndexOf();
includes();
startsWith()
endsWith()
match();
matchAll()   // returns iterator
replace();
replaceAll();
repeat();
slice(start, end)
substring(start, end)
substr(start, length)
split()
search(regex)
trim()
eval(); (eval() is from Window Object)
localeCompare()

N.p:- substring() cannot accept negative indexes. slice() does.
